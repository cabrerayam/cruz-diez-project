Contact Us

Copyright

Do you have questions about Cruz-Diez’s copyright? Head to our contact page and we will gladly help you get all the answers.

 

Artwork Restoration

Cruz-Diez’s studios are in charge of the conservation and restoration of his work.

 

Catalogue Raisonné

All institutions and collectors can get their work by Cruz-Diez registered in the Catalogue Raisonné. You can help us retrace history by contacting us!

Press

We are happy to answer any questions and provide more information about Cruz-Diez for any media and press coverage.

 

Artwork Authentication

The Cruz-Diez Foundation is entitled to confirm exclusive authorship for Cruz-Diez’s works of art attributed to Cruz-Diez. Contact us if you own an artwork and would like to acquire a certificate.

 

Documentation Centers

Cruz-Diez documentation center provides full assistance to curators, historians, students and cultural institutions worldwide.

 

News

ARCO Madrid

Galeria Cayón has its booth at Spain’s International Contemporary Art Fair ARCO and presents one Cruz-Diez’s mesmerizing piece

Art in Motion at Springfield Art Museum

Cruz-Diez’s work will be pulled from the Springfield Art Museum’s collection for its exhibition Art in Motion, presented in collaboration with Missouri State University.

Mouvement | Denise René

The Bonisson Art Center is currently presenting a tribute to Denise René, Parisian iconic gallery owner and long-time friend of Cruz-Diez.

Chromosaturation at PAMM

Chromosaturation, 1965/2007 was acquired for PAMM’s collection in 2020 and is now on view in the museum.

Un Patrimonio a perpetuar

A documentary exhibition on the monumental heritage of Carlos Cruz-Diez.

The Euphoria of Color

The Euphoria of Color marks the first-year anniversary of Galleria Continua space at Burj Al Arab Jumeirah, and the return of Carlos Cruz-Diez’s œuvre after the recent feature of his immersive artwork Chromosaturation at France pavilion of Expo 2020 Dubai.

Paris + by Art Basel

For the first edition of the Paris+ by Art Basel art fair, Galleria Continua will present Colonne Chromointerférente, a monumental artwork by Cruz-Diez at the heart of the Tuileries garden.

L'occhio in gioco

This playful exhibition explores how artists have developped many techniques and stratagems to deceive the eyes, from the Middle Ages until today.

Paris et nulle part Ailleurs

In 1960, when Cruz-Diez settled in Paris with his family, the city was the world capital of the arts. This exhibition retraces the trajectory of 24 international artists that chose Paris to develop their art.

Frieze Masters, London

Galleria Continua presents a Cruz-Diez solo show at Frieze Masters, in London, highlighting the artist’s tireless inventiveness and search for technical reliability since 1959.

El Peso de la Forma

Displayed at the Museo Nacional Centro de Arte Reina Sofía in Madrid in 2021, this exhibition about Cruz-Diez's graphic work travels to Caracas, Venezuela. Organized by the Archivo Fotografía Urbana and the Cruz-Diez Foundation, the exhibition offers a survey of Cruz-Diez's graphic creativity.

Chromosaturation at MOCA, LA

Our House, Selection from MOCA's collection features a Chromosaturation, acquired by the museum in 2012

Dynamic Eye

Hosted by the Museum of Art Pudong (Shanghai), the exhibition brings together nearly 120 artworks from the Tate collection.

Free Color

Directed by Alberto Arvelo, the documentary explores the Cruz-Diez's history and creative process, and documents what became his life's quest: to free color from form.

Venet Foundation

Carlos Cruz-Diez makes his entrance to the park of the Venet Foundation, in the south of France with Environnement de Transchromie Circulaire (2017)

The Ability to Dream

This group exhibition is the first collaboration between Cruz-Diez and Galleria Continua.
